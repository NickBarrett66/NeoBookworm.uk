## What you need to keep on top of

Two things, both small:

### 1. Renew your web address once a year

You'll get an email from your registrar (the company your web address is registered with) about a month before it's due to renew. Pay it, and you're sorted for another year. You should already have the login details — they were emailed to you when the address was first registered.

If you can't find that email or you're not sure who your registrar is, message me and I'll help you track it down. This is important: if the renewal lapses, you lose the web address, and someone else can buy it.

Tip: most registrars let you turn on **auto-renew**, so it just charges your card each year and you never have to think about it. I'd recommend doing that.

### 2. Log in to Netlify once or twice a year

Just to check your account is still on the free Starter plan and nothing's changed. You don't need to do anything else — Netlify will email you if there's ever a problem.
