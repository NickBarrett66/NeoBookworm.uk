Common questions

Do I need to book, or can I just turn up?

Just turn up — no appointment needed for most jobs. If you want to be sure we've got your tyre size in, give us a quick call first on 01249 490498.

Do you fit tyres I've bought myself?

Yes. If you've sourced your own tyres, bring them in and we'll fit them. We can also swap a pair from the back to the front for you.

Do you sell part-worn tyres?

No — we only sell new tyres. We can source tyres for all budgets, plus premium brands like Michelin, Pirelli and Continental, and commercial or specialist sizes too.

Is the wheel alignment check really free?

Yes. Come in for a free alignment check — if it doesn't need doing, there's no charge, and we'll never try to sell you an alignment you don't need.

Tyres

We stock a wide range to suit all budgets, and can source hard-to-find, commercial and specialist tyres too. Premium brands like Michelin, Pirelli and Continental are no problem.

We only sell new tyres — no part-worn. Sourced your own? Bring them in and we'll fit them. And we'll always be honest about the life left in your current tyres — we won't change a tyre that doesn't need changing.

Wheel alignment

Our new digital 4-post alignment system has aligned over 1,000 wheels since we opened. Misaligned wheels wear your tyres out faster and pull the car to one side — getting it right saves you money and drives better.

Come in for a free alignment check. If it doesn't need doing, there's no charge — and we won't try to sell you one you don't need.

Wheel balancing

Every wheel is balanced properly after fitting — for a smooth ride, no steering wobble and even tyre wear.

Brakes

Let us do your brake pads and discs — supplied and fitted for most cars at excellent prices, quality parts only.

We're happy to check your brakes for free and give you honest advice on whether they need replacing yet.

Exhausts & batteries

Exhaust repairs and full replacements at competitive prices. We only use excellent-quality parts, so the repair lasts.

Flat battery? We supply and fit car batteries while you wait — quick, and no appointment needed.

Puncture repair

Where a tyre can safely be repaired, we'll repair it rather than sell you a new one. If it's not safe to save, we'll tell you straight.
