Why Beckett Lofts exists

Rob started Beckett Lofts in 2019 after years working on larger projects where responsibility got diluted—survey by one person, quote by another, build “managed” by someone who isn’t there.

He set up Beckett Lofts so the person you meet at the start is the same person turning the key in the door at the end.
