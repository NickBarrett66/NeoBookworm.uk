Worth saying upfront

When you probably shouldn’t switch.

If your current site is fast, works properly on a phone, brings you work, and you own it outright — keep it. There’s no case for switching just for the sake of it. This page is for electricians whose site is doing one of these specific things:

  • The domain lapsed and now shows a parking page when people search for you
  • You’re on Wix or Squarespace and paying monthly rent for something you don’t own
  • Someone else manages it and you’re not sure the domain is even in your name
  • You have a real site but something specific is costing you jobs — no registration number, no working contact form, loads slowly on a phone

If none of that applies, you’re probably fine. If any of it does, read on.
