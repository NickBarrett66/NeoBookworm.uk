Why it matters

When something leaks, people choose a plumber fast — usually on their phone.

Most plumbing enquiries start with a quick search: a boiler that’s packed up, a leak that’s getting worse, a radiator that won’t heat. They check two or three options and ring the one that feels trustworthy.

Your site only has to do a few things well:

  • Make it easy to act. Clear phone number, clear contact option, and no fiddly layout on mobile.
  • Show you’re legit. Gas Safe (if applicable), where you cover, and the kind of work you actually do.
  • Back it up with proof. A few job photos and reviews so they feel confident choosing you.

That’s how you win the urgent call-outs and the bigger jobs — without paying commission or competing in a quote race.

A plumber in your area with a website and a Google listing will typically start picking up enquiries they couldn’t reach before: the homeowner who just moved in, the landlord without a regular plumber, the emergency caller who searches and rings the first credible result they see.
