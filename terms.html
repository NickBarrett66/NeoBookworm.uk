The website build

Fixed price £299

The £299 fixed price covers the design, build, and launch of your new website. That means:

  • A multi-page site built around your trade and how you work
  • Your services, contact details, and the areas you cover
  • A design that looks right on phones, tablets, and desktop
  • Setup on hosting so it is ready for people to find
  • Your web address (e.g. nicksplumbing.co.uk) — most cost £10–15/year and that's included. If your preferred name is taken I'll suggest alternatives at no extra cost
  • Changes included until you're happy — you review the site before it goes live and I make any adjustments you need

You don't pay until you're happy with it. I'll show you the finished site before asking for payment. If it's not right for you, there's no obligation to proceed and the site won't go live.

Once payment is made the site goes live and it's yours outright — no hidden costs, no ongoing obligations unless you choose to add the care plan.

Monthly care plan (optional)

£9.99 / month

The care plan is entirely your choice — your site stays live either way. It is there if you would rather I handle the technical side so you can focus on the work you are actually paid for.

It covers:

  • Keeping your site live, fast, and on the right hosting
  • Security and software updates as they come out
  • Small text and content changes (up to around 30 minutes of work a month)
  • Your annual web address renewal — I sort it so it stays registered in your name (typically £8–15/year to the registrar, covered while you're on the plan)

Bigger changes — new pages, new sections, new features — are quoted separately at £25 an hour. I will always tell you the cost before starting.

You can cancel at any time with no fuss. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing month, and your site files are yours regardless.
