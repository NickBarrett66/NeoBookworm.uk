1. Who we are

We're Smart Tyres Gloucester, a tyre fitting depot based at Unit 1, 28 Hempsted Lane, Hempsted, Gloucester GL2 5JA, supplying new and part-worn tyres and fitting.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, Smart Tyres Gloucester is the data controller for personal information collected through this website. That means we decide what we collect and why, and we're responsible for keeping it safe.

2. What we collect and why

We only collect information that helps us do the job — getting back to you about a tyre or a visit. Here's what we collect and why.

2a. Enquiry form

When you fill in the enquiry form on our site, we collect:

Information Why we collect it
Your name So we know who we're talking to when we get back to you
Your phone number or email address To reply with an answer, a price, or to arrange a time to come in
Your message (what you need) To understand your tyre size or job and give you a useful answer

We collect this information to respond to your enquiry. The legal basis is legitimate interests — both yours (getting a quick, useful reply) and ours (running our business).

Your enquiry is sent to us by email. NeoBookworm, who built and host this site, deliver the message to us on our behalf and don't use it for any other purpose. We don't keep it in a separate database — it lives in our inbox and is deleted once the conversation is resolved and we no longer need it.

2b. Directions map

Our "Finding the depot" section includes an interactive map showing the route to our unit. This map uses Stadia Maps for the map tiles (the background images that make up the map). When the map loads, your browser makes a request to Stadia Maps' servers, which means your IP address is sent to them as part of that request.

We don't deliberately share any personal information with Stadia Maps — an IP address is sent as a side effect of how the web works. The map only loads when you scroll down to it. Stadia Maps' privacy policy is at stadiamaps.com/privacy-policy.

3. Who receives your information

We don't sell, rent or share your information with any marketing companies or third parties who would use it for their own purposes. Here are the organisations that handle your data on our behalf:

Organisation What they do with your data
NeoBookworm (the company that built and hosts this site) Provides the website. Enquiry emails are forwarded through their infrastructure before reaching us. They don't use your data for any purpose other than making the site work for us.
Stadia Maps Serves the background map images when the directions map on this page loads. Receives your IP address as part of normal web requests.

4. How long we keep your information

Enquiry emails (name, contact details, message) are kept in our inbox until the conversation is resolved and we no longer need them, then deleted — typically within 6–12 months.

5. Your rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have a number of rights over your personal information:

Access

You can ask us for a copy of any personal information we hold about you.

Correction

You can ask us to correct any information we have that's wrong or out of date.

Deletion

You can ask us to delete your information where we no longer need it.

Objection

You can object to us using your information where the legal basis is legitimate interests. We'll stop unless we have a compelling reason to continue.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in section 7. We'll respond within one month.

If you're not happy with how we handle your request — or with our use of your data generally — you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). You can contact them at ico.org.uk or on 0303 123 1113. We'd always prefer to hear from you first so we can try to resolve things directly.

6. Cookies

This site doesn't use any tracking or analytics cookies. It doesn't set any cookies of its own.
