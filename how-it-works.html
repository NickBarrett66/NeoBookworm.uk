How it works

One clear build.
Your choice after that.

Every client gets the same thing — a 5-page website that works on phones, looks great, and helps you appear in Google search lists. You don’t pay until you’re happy. After that, you either have me keep it running for £9.99 a month, or you take it over yourself. If you need bigger changes later, email me and I’ll quote before I start.

No pressure, no upselling. The £299 build is deliberately complete — most tradespeople never need anything more to win work online. If your business changes down the line, I’m still here for ad-hoc help or bigger projects, always with a clear price first.
