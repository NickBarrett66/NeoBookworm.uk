The process

From first contact
to live website in days

No lengthy briefs, no endless back-and-forth. Just a simple process, a custom build, and a site you're proud of.

1

I research your trade

I look at what your local competitors are doing online, what your customers are typing into Google, and what makes you worth calling. Your site is built around that — not a blank template with your name dropped in.

2

I build your site

A custom 5-page website — Home, About, Services, Gallery, Contact. Designed to look great on phones (where most people will find you), and written so Google knows exactly what you do and where you work.

3

You have a look and tell me what to change

I send you a link so you can see it before it goes live. Tell me anything you’d like changed — wording, colours, photos — and I’ll sort it. Once you’re happy, you pay the £299 and I make it live on your own web address.

4

I keep it ticking over — or hand it to you

Your choice: for £19.99 a month I keep the site live, secure and up to date — just message me if you need a change. Or I can hand it over completely and show you how to manage it yourself.
