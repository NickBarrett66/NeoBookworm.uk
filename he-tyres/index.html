Locking wheel nut removal

How mobile fitting works

Getting new tyres fitted at your door takes three steps

1

Get in touch

Call us or send a message with your tyre size, vehicle type and location. We'll give you a price and book a slot that works for you.

2

We come to you

Our mobile van turns up at your home or workplace. No waiting rooms, no arranging a lift, no disruption to your day.

3

Job done

Your new tyres are fitted, balanced, and your TPMS is checked and reset. You're back on the road in no time.

A family business that cares about doing it right

HE Tyres is a family-run business based in Swindon. We believe tyre fitting should be straightforward, fairly priced, and genuinely convenient — which means fitting your tyres where it suits you, not where it suits us.

Whether you're at home, at work, or stuck at the roadside, our mobile service means you're never left scrambling for a way to get to a garage. And if you'd rather drop in, our Westmead depot is open Monday to Saturday.

We keep our prices competitive, our service professional, and our customers coming back.
