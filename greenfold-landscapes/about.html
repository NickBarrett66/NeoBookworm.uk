About

A small team that treats your garden like it’s our own.

Greenfold came together after years of crossing paths on larger Wiltshire landscaping jobs — two lead landscapers who preferred straight answers, tidy sites, and gardens that still look right seasons later.

Our story

We formalised the partnership in 2019 after running parallel crews for different contractors — same county, same standards, and a shared dislike of vague quotes. Between us there’s over 20 years of combined landscaping experience, mostly patios, driveways and full garden redesigns around Swindon and Wiltshire.

Work has come from referrals, repeat customers, and the usual trade directories — not because we’re the loudest name online, but because we keep the basics solid: clear pricing, realistic schedules, and proper groundwork that doesn’t come back to haunt you a winter later.
