Services

The jobs we’re known for (and the ones we’re happy to help with).

We’re landscapers first — but if it’s part of getting your garden finished, we’ll tell you straight what we can do and what we’ll refer out.

Core landscaping services

Solid groundwork. Clean lines. A garden that holds up.

Patios & paving

Natural stone, porcelain and block-paved patios — laid on a proper sub-base with the right falls and drainage. We excavate to the correct depth, compact and lay a solid foundation, then finish with crisp jointing and clean edges that stay put.

Good to know: The bit you can’t see — the sub-base and falls — is what stops a patio sinking or puddling later.

Driveways

Block-paved, resin-bound and gravel driveways, excavated and built to take the weight. We dig out, lay a structural sub-base and edge restraints, and finish to a clean, level surface with proper drainage so it lasts for years.

Good to know: We’ll advise on permeable options and drainage to keep things compliant and puddle-free.

Decking & pergolas

Timber and composite decking, pergolas and sleeper beds — built level, solid and squeak-free. Properly set posts and frames mean a deck that stays flat and safe underfoot, with a finish that suits the rest of the garden.

Good to know: Composite costs a little more up front but barely needs maintaining — we’ll talk you through both.

Fencing & garden walls

Fencing, gates, screening and garden walls — sturdy, plumb and neatly finished. Posts set in concrete, lines run true, and brickwork or sleeper walls built to hold ground and last.

Good to know: We’ll sort levels and retaining where a garden slopes, not just stand panels up and hope.

Turfing & planting

New lawns, artificial grass, borders and planting schemes that establish and last. We prepare the ground properly — levelled, fed and weed-free — so turf knits in and planting thrives rather than struggles.

Good to know: Artificial grass is a great option for shaded or high-traffic gardens where real turf won’t thrive.

Full garden redesigns (premium)

Start-to-finish transformations — designed and built as one project. From an overgrown plot to a finished garden: design, groundwork, hard landscaping and planting, all project-managed by one team so it comes together as a whole.

Good to know: This is our standout service — bring us a tired or bare garden and a budget, and we’ll show you what’s possible.

Additional services

If it helps get the job finished properly, we’ll usually help — or point you to the right trade.

  • Garden maintenance: One-off tidy-ups, clearances and regular upkeep on contract.
  • Drainage & soakaways: Land drains and soakaways to deal with standing water.
  • Garden lighting & power: Outdoor lighting and weatherproof sockets (electrics by a Part P contractor).
  • Pressure washing: Bringing tired patios, paths and driveways back to life.

What we don’t do

We don’t take on tree surgery, mains drainage or major structural retaining walls on our own. If your job needs a qualified arborist, groundworks contractor or structural engineer alongside the landscaping, we’ll say so early so the project runs smoothly.
